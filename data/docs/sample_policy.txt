# OmniMart Retail Company Policies & Information

## 1. Return & Refund Policy

1.1 Standard Returns
Customers may return most new, unopened items sold and fulfilled by OmniMart within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.
During the holidays, items shipped by OmniMart between November 1 and December 31 can be returned until January 31 of the following year.
Return shipping is free for standard returns using our provided prepaid labels. If you choose to use your own shipping method, you will be responsible for the cost.
Refunds are typically processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your return.

1.2 Electronics Returns
Electronics (including laptops, tablets, smartphones, and cameras) must be returned within 14 days of purchase.
All electronics must include original packaging, manuals, and accessories.
If a device has been opened but is not defective, a 15% restocking fee will apply.
Defective electronics can be exchanged for the exact same item within 30 days of purchase without a restocking fee.
Apple products follow a strict 14-day return window, opened or unopened.

1.3 Non-Returnable Items
The following items cannot be returned:
- Gift cards and prepaid game cards.
- Downloadable software products.
- Opened unsealed software or video games (can only be exchanged for the same title if defective).
- Personal care items (toothbrushes, shavers) that have been opened.
- Custom-made or personalized products.

1.4 Damaged or Defective Items
If you receive a defective or damaged item, please contact our support team within 48 hours of delivery.
We will arrange for a free replacement or full refund, including original shipping costs.

## 2. Shipping & Delivery Methods

2.1 Standard Shipping
Standard shipping is free on all orders over $50.
For orders under $50, standard shipping costs $5.99.
Delivery typically takes 5-7 business days within the contiguous United States.
Orders are processed within 1-2 business days before shipping.

2.2 Expedited Shipping
2-Day Shipping is available for $12.99 per order.
Next-Day Delivery is available for $19.99 per order.
Expedited orders must be placed before 2:00 PM EST to be shipped the same day. Orders placed after 2:00 PM EST will ship the following business day.

2.3 International Shipping
We currently ship to Canada, Mexico, the UK, and the EU.
International standard shipping takes 10-21 business days and costs a flat rate of $25.00.
Customers are responsible for any customs duties, import taxes, or brokerage fees applied by the destination country.
We do not offer expedited shipping for international orders.

2.4 P.O. Boxes and APO/FPO
We ship to P.O. Boxes and APO/FPO addresses using USGS Standard tracking. Expedited shipping is not available for these addresses.
Delivery to APO/FPO addresses may take up to 45 days.

## 3. Warranties & Repairs

3.1 OmniMart Hardware Warranty
All OmniMart house-brand electronics come with a 1-year limited manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship.
This warranty does not cover accidental damage (drops, spills), theft, or normal wear and tear.
To file a warranty claim, you must provide your original receipt or order number.

3.2 Extended Protection Plans
Customers can purchase OmniProtect extended warranties for eligible electronics and appliances within 30 days of the original purchase.
OmniProtect covers accidental damage (such as screen cracks and liquid damage) from day one.
OmniProtect plans last for 2 or 3 years depending on the tier purchased.
Repairs under OmniProtect require a $49 deductible for smartphones and laptops, and a $29 deductible for tablets and smartwatches.

3.3 Third-Party Manufacturer Warranties
Items produced by third-party manufacturers (e.g., Sony, Samsung, LG) carry their respective manufacturer warranties.
OmniMart does not process these warranty claims after the standard 30-day return window; customers must contact the manufacturer directly.

3.4 Professional Repair Services
OmniMart offers in-store repair services for smartphones, laptops, and desktop computers.
Diagnostic fees are $35, which will be waived if you choose to proceed with the repair.
Battery replacements for smartphones start at $69.99.
Screen repairs for smartphones start at $129.99.
All OmniMart repairs come with a 90-day parts and labor guarantee.

## 4. Payment Methods & Pricing

4.1 Accepted Payment Methods
We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover credit/debit cards.
Digital wallets including Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal are accepted during online checkout.
We do not accept personal checks or money orders.

4.2 Store Credit and Gift Cards
OmniMart Gift Cards do not expire and have no maintenance fees.
Store credit issued for returns without a receipt is valid for 1 year from the date of issue.
Gift cards cannot be redeemed for cash unless required by law.

4.3 Price Matching
We offer price matching for identical items sold by recognized major competitors (e.g., Target, Walmart, Best Buy, Amazon).
The item must be in stock at the competitor at the time of the request.
We do not price match items sold by third-party marketplace sellers on Amazon or Walmart.com.
We do not price match special events (Black Friday, Cyber Monday, clearance sales, or limited-quantity offers).

4.4 Sales Tax
Sales tax is automatically calculated based on the shipping destination according to local and state laws.

## 5. OmniMart Rewards Program

5.1 Earning Points
Members earn 1 point for every $1 spent on qualifying purchases.
Bonus points are offered during special promotional periods.
Points are credited to your account 30 days after the purchase.

5.2 Redeeming Points
Every 100 points can be redeemed for a $5 OmniMart reward certificate.
Reward certificates expire 60 days after they are issued.
Points themselves do not expire as long as there is purchasing activity on the account at least once every 12 months.

5.3 Elite Tier Status
Customers who spend over $1,000 in a calendar year automatically upgrade to the Elite Tier.
Elite Tier benefits include free 2-Day Shipping on all orders (no minimum), extended 60-day return window (except for electronics), and access to exclusive flash sales.

## 6. Privacy & Security

6.1 Data Collection
We collect personal information such as name, shipping address, email, and payment details to process orders.
We use cookies on our website to remember your preferences and shopping cart items.

6.2 Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal data to third-party marketers.
We share data with trusted third parties only to the extent necessary to fulfill your order (e.g., FedEx, UPS, Stripe payment processor).

6.3 Account Security
We recommend using strong, unique passwords for your OmniMart account.
Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is available and recommended for all accounts.
If you suspect unauthorized access, contact support@omnimart.example.com immediately.

## 8. Product Categories and Specific Details

8.1 Video Games and Consoles
Consoles can be returned within 30 days if unopened. If opened, they are subject to a 15% restocking fee.
Digital game codes are final sale and cannot be refunded under any circumstances.
Used games can be returned within 7 days for a full refund.

8.2 Appliances (Large)
Large appliances (refrigerators, washers, dryers) cannot be returned in-store. They require a scheduled pickup for returns.
A $50 pickup fee applies for returning non-defective large appliances.
Installation fees paid at the time of purchase are non-refundable.

8.3 Furniture
Furniture must be returned unassembled and in its original packaging within 30 days.
Assembled furniture cannot be returned unless it arrived damaged.

8.4 Apparel and Clothing
Clothing and shoes must be unworn, unwashed, and have the original tags attached to be eligible for a return.
Intimate apparel, swimwear, and earrings are final sale for hygiene reasons.
